Guard-Gated Golf Community

Private Living in the Henderson Foothills

Anthem Country Club offers a lifestyle defined by privacy, scenic views, and a sense of calm that's hard to find elsewhere in the Las Vegas Valley. Tucked into the foothills of Henderson, this guard-gated community is known for its championship golf course setting, elevated terrain, and thoughtfully designed neighborhoods.

With a mix of custom homes, panoramic views, and country club amenities, Anthem Country Club attracts buyers looking for a more refined residential experience without sacrificing everyday convenience.

Buyer's Guide

5 Things Every Home Buyer
Should Know

5 Things to Know

01
Lifestyle & Community Atmosphere

A guard-gated community built around privacy, security, and an upscale environment — attracting executives, retirees, and relocation buyers who value a refined lifestyle.

  • Championship golf course at Anthem Country Club
  • Fitness center and wellness facilities
  • Tennis and pickleball courts
  • Resort-style pool and dining events
  • Active social calendar for residents
02
Schools Serving Anthem Country Club

Served by Clark County School District with well-regarded public options and strong private and charter alternatives. Coronado High School is particularly known for academics and athletics.

  • Frank Lamping & Wallin Elementary Schools
  • Del E. Webb Middle School
  • Coronado High School (AP programs)
  • Pinecrest Academy of Nevada (private)
  • Henderson International & Coral Academy
03
Real Estate Market & Home Styles

Primarily single-family semi-custom and custom homes. Established inventory and limited supply mean well-presented homes attract strong buyer interest.

  • Mid-range homes from the $600,000s
  • Larger homes $800,000 to $1.5M+
  • Golf course & custom homes exceed $1.5M
  • City, Strip, and mountain view lots
  • Golf course frontage vs. interior lots
04
Location & Accessibility

Private residential feel with easy access to Henderson's medical corridor, shopping, and the Las Vegas Strip via Eastern Ave, St. Rose Pkwy, and I-215.

  • Harry Reid International Airport ~20 min
  • Las Vegas Strip ~20–25 min
  • Henderson medical corridor ~10–15 min
  • The District at Green Valley Ranch ~15 min
  • Grocery, fitness & medical offices nearby
05
Dining, Shopping & Local Favorites

Minutes from Henderson's top restaurants, The District at Green Valley Ranch, and outdoor parks — with the community's own club dining right at the doorstep.

  • Ventano Italian Grill & Seafood (Strip views)
  • Settebello Pizzeria & North Italia
  • Echo & Rig Steakhouse
  • The District at Green Valley Ranch
  • Anthem Hills Park & Henderson trail network

Common Questions

Frequently Asked
Questions

Anthem Country Club is considered one of Henderson's most desirable communities due to its guard-gated setting, golf course environment, and access to amenities. The elevated terrain, maintained streets, and country club lifestyle make it a consistently sought-after address for buyers who prioritize privacy and quality of life.

The community primarily features single-family homes, including semi-custom and custom properties with a variety of layouts, lot sizes, and views. Some homes offer golf course frontage, city and Strip views, mountain views, and private outdoor living spaces — no two properties are quite the same.

Yes, Anthem Country Club is a guard-gated community with controlled access and HOA guidelines that maintain community standards. The entrance is staffed and access is restricted to residents and their guests, which contributes to the community's sense of security and exclusivity.

Club membership is typically optional, but many residents choose to join for access to golf, dining, fitness, and social amenities. For many residents, the club becomes part of their daily routine — whether that's morning workouts, evening dinners, or weekend social events.

Home prices vary based on location, views, and upgrades. Mid-range homes start in the $600,000s, with larger homes ranging from $800,000 to $1.5M+. Golf course and elevated view homes typically command higher values, and premium custom properties often exceed $1.5M.

Inventory can be limited, and well-maintained homes with desirable features often attract strong buyer interest. Because the community is established and not expanding, supply is naturally constrained — buyers who find the right home typically move with confidence and purpose.
