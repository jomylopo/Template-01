Why Henderson?

Nevada's Most Livable City

Henderson consistently ranks among the safest and most desirable cities in the entire United States. With top-rated schools, world-class parks, and proximity to the Las Vegas Strip, Henderson offers the rare combination of suburban calm and urban convenience.

Whether you're drawn to the private golf communities of MacDonald Highlands, the lakeside resort lifestyle of Lake Las Vegas, or the modern master-planned neighborhoods of Inspirada and Cadence — Henderson has a community for every lifestyle and budget.

The Crighton Rinaldi Team has deep roots in Henderson real estate and can guide you through every neighborhood, price point, and opportunity the market has to offer.
