Usage Rules
Cyan Teal (#33dbe7) — Primary CTAs, accent lines, overlines, stat numbers, hover states, borders
Midnight (#021C1E) — Page background, footer, nav, dark section backgrounds
Pearl White (#EDEFF0) — Body text on dark backgrounds, hero headlines
Soft Teal / Mint (#7acfd1, #bcdecd) — Supporting accents, card borders, tag labels
Sage (#ededc2) — Highlight backgrounds, callout sections, warm accent areas
Ocean Deep / Deep Slate — Card backgrounds, secondary dark surfaces
